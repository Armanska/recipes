INGREDIENTS:
- 1.5 liters of chicken broth,
- about 150 g of tomato paste,
- about 50 ml of sweet cream 30-36%,
- chopped parsley,
- pasta (approx. 50 g per person) or rice (approx. 30 g per person),
- salt, ground black pepper

PREPARATION:
Cook the rice or pasta according to the instructions on the package.
Heat the broth. Add tomato paste and stir to dissolve. Boil. (It is best to add the concentrate gradually until the soup has the right sourness and color).
Finally, stir in the sour cream. Season to taste with salt and pepper if desired.
Serve with cooked rice or pasta. Sprinkle with chopped parsley.
