INGREDIENTS/ 4 SERVINGS:
- 2 carrots,
- 0.5 kg of potatoes,
- 150 g green beans,
- 250 g of cauliflower,
- 1/2 onion,
- 1 tablespoon of butter,
- 1/2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil,
- 1.5 liters of vegetable or chicken broth,
- 1/3 cup of cream 18% or 30%,
- dill or parsley or basil.

PREPARATION:
In a wide, larger pot, fry the diced onion in butter and olive oil. 
Add the peeled and sliced carrot and fry, stirring, for about 3 minutes.
Add peeled and diced potatoes and fry again for about 3 minutes.
Add hot broth and bring to a boil. Cook covered for about 5 minutes.
Add the beans trimmed at the ends and cut into pieces and the cauliflower florets. 
Season with salt and freshly ground pepper.
Cook covered for about 15 minutes until the vegetables are soft. 
Finally, add chopped dill (or parsley or basil leaves).
Remove the soup from the heat and season with cream 
(first, mix it with gradually added brew, in the amount of about a glass, 
then pour it into the pot with the soup and mix). Serve with bread.
